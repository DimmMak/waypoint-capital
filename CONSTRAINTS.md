# 📐 Constraints

> The rules that define what Blue Hill Capital CAN and CANNOT do.
> Non-negotiable. Violation is a process failure.

---

## 🏦 Account structure

```
Type:           Traditional IRA (Chase J.P. Morgan Self-Directed)
Capital:        $7,000 (verified 2026-04-17)
Tax treatment:  Tax-deferred until withdrawal
Access:         Locked until age 59½ (10% penalty + income tax if early)
```

---

## ✅ What we CAN do

- Buy stocks, ETFs, ADRs freely
- Cash-secured puts (sell puts with cash to cover — once options enabled)
- Covered calls (sell calls against owned stock)
- Long calls, long puts (defined risk)
- Vertical spreads (defined risk)
- Hold for any timeframe, tax-free compounding

## ❌ What we CANNOT do

- ❌ Short-sell stocks (IRA prohibits)
- ❌ Naked short options (uncovered risk blocked)
- ❌ Margin / leverage (Reg T bars retirement accounts)
- ❌ Early withdrawal without penalty (10% + tax under 59½)

## 🎯 Sizing rules (ironclad)

```
MAX single position:     20% of account ($1,400)
MAX positions open:      4-5 concurrent
MIN cash reserve:        15% at all times ($1,050)
MAX drawdown threshold:  -15% account → full reassessment of every holding
```

### Position sizing ladder

| Conviction | Judge label | Capital | Shares of $100 stock | Shares of $75 stock |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| STRONG BUY | Full | $1,400 | 14 | 18 |
| BUY | Half | $700 | 7 | 9 |
| HOLD | Quarter | $350 | 3-4 | 4 |
| HOLD (low conv) | Starter | $175 | 1-2 | 2 |
| SELL/STRONG SELL | Pass | $0 | 0 | 0 |

**First 6 months: always size DOWN one rung from Judge's call.** Discipline tax on first-year psychology.

---

## 🛡️ Stop-loss discipline

```
Hard stop:             2x ATR below entry  OR  thesis invalidation (whichever first)
Trailing stop:         moves up with price; never down
Portfolio circuit:     -15% account drawdown triggers mandatory pause
Review trigger:        any stop hit → mandatory post-mortem in trading-journal
```

**Stops are not suggestions.** Every trade has one documented BEFORE entry.

---

## 📝 Trade entry checklist (must satisfy ALL before placing order)

```
[ ] Rumble completed with structured footer
[ ] User hypothesis pre-registered (or explicit --skip)
[ ] Judge verdict reviewed, user-vs-Judge divergence understood
[ ] Position size matches framework (Judge size − 1 rung in first 6mo)
[ ] Stop price defined and written in trading-journal.md
[ ] Entry zone priced via limit order (GTC preferred)
[ ] Invalidation triggers documented (thesis-break conditions)
[ ] 30d/60d/90d check-in dates scheduled
```

Missing any box? **Don't trade.**

---

## 🚫 Forbidden behaviors

```
❌ Chasing at all-time highs without pullback
❌ Adding to losers without new information
❌ Averaging down to "fix" a broken thesis
❌ Buying a name with no written thesis
❌ Selling a winner because it "feels extended" without a trigger
❌ Revenge-trading after a loss
❌ Deploying >20% into any single position
❌ Breaking the 15% cash minimum
❌ Holding a position past its documented stop
❌ Trading outside market hours except GTC limit orders
```

**Every forbidden behavior that is violated becomes a written post-mortem.** Even small violations logged. Pattern detection over time is the lesson.
