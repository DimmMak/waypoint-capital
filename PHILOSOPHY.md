# 🧬 Philosophy

> The 10 principles that govern Blue Hill Capital.
> Updated rarely. Overridden never.

---

## 1. 🏗️ Process > parameters

Perfect weights applied emotionally always lose to imperfect weights applied with discipline. We don't tweak parameters chasing yesterday's outcome. We honor the process and let data update the parameters on its own schedule.

## 2. 🔒 Sealed rooms

Analysts cannot see the PM's hypothesis. Implemented as *subagent isolation* — each of the 13 legends runs in a fresh context that has no access to the parent session. Blindness is physical, not discipline-based. (The v0.6 → v0.7 fix is documented in the royal-rumble repo.)

## 3. 📌 Pre-registration

Every hypothesis is logged **before** catalysts play out — with a timestamp, conviction level, falsification condition. Post-fact rationalization is the retail disease. Pre-registration is the cure.

## 4. 📎 Cite or abstain

Every specific number in every analysis carries a source tag: `[SRC: S1]` for a cited web search, `[REPORTED]` for public filings, `[ESTIMATE]` when tagged as such, `[UNVERIFIED]` when honestly acknowledged as a gap. Never "roughly X." Never "I think."

## 5. 🛡️ Fabrication Guard

Automated pattern-scan for invented specifics — analyst targets without named analyst, moving averages without chart source, TAM figures without cited report, private-conversation quotes. Flagged claims surface in verdicts. Discipline enforced by machine, not memory.

## 6. 🌱 Earn your features

No skill, no trade, no allocation ships until pain is real and repeated. Speculative building is how retail investors accumulate tools instead of returns. Trigger conditions are non-negotiable.

## 7. ⚖️ Risk × reward matrix on every decision

Before any ship, build, or trade: rate the decision on a 3×3 matrix (Risk L/M/H × Reward L/M/H) plus Effort tiebreaker. T1 = ship / T2 = wait for signal / T3 = kill. Applied every decision, always visible.

## 8. 🗄️ Audit trail is the moat

Every rumble → `data/predictions.json`. Every strategy meeting → `data/strategy-meetings.json`. Every comparison → `data/comparisons.json`. Every trade → `trades/*.md`. Every retro → `reviews/*.md`. Git-logged, timestamped, unfalsifiable. **Our track record is the product.**

## 9. 🎯 Real-life role naming

Every skill, every agent, every role is named after a real corporate function — Research Associate, Performance Analyst, Events Desk, Research Director. Loads massive context instantly. Descriptive names (*future-planner*, *task-router*) are replaced. Fund roles mirror how real funds are organized.

## 10. 🔬 Research discipline (3 non-negotiable rules)

Every human-written note follows the same 3 rules professional hedge fund analysts use:
1. **Idea folders > source folders** — organize by TICKER, not by source
2. **Every memo has a bear case** — even (especially) when bullish
3. **Every claim that can be challenged gets a tag** — `[SRC: ...]`, `[REPORTED]`, `[ESTIMATE]`, `[UNVERIFIED]`

Full spec with examples + anti-fatigue fallback in [RESEARCH-DISCIPLINE.md](RESEARCH-DISCIPLINE.md).

## 11. 🔮 Future-proof by default

Before shipping anything, ask: "Will this survive and stay readable 12 months from now?" Version stamps. CHANGELOG entries. One source of truth per fact. Plain text > proprietary format. Names that age well.

---

## 🎭 What we are NOT

- ❌ Chasing momentum at ATHs
- ❌ Cutting corners on cite-or-abstain "for speed"
- ❌ Over-sizing based on conviction alone
- ❌ Bypassing pre-registration
- ❌ Using data we can't verify
- ❌ Tweaking weights based on last week's news
- ❌ Promising returns we haven't earned
- ❌ Managing outside capital until the track record earns it

---

## 💎 The deepest principle

**You don't beat the market with better data. You beat the market with better discipline.**

Blue Hill is a discipline-first fund. The data is free. The discipline is earned daily.
